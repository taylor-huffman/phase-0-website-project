Back Story

Web development began for me in my early 20's purely out of necessity. As a struggling musician playing in a band that needed a website, I stepped up to the challenge and quickly grew to love web design and development.

But as life would happen, dreams of becoming a rock star and web developer soon were put on hold...

Fast forward 15 years and here we are in the present...

After an extended journey away from my passions I'm finally back where I belong, working as a WordPress Web Developer for the past 2 years and readying myself to enter the Flatiron School Software Engineering boot camp to further grow my skill set...

...and I couldn't be happier.
